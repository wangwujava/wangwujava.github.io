ABOUT US:

BorderlessAd is a leading mobile advertising technology company focused on user acquisition and retargeting. Founded in Hong Kong in 2020 by a team of seasoned marketing specialists, we have built a powerful digital ad platform to elevate performance for mobile applications across the globe.

Drawing on our in-depth expertise in ad tech, we provide end-to-end solutions covering campaign deployment, ongoing optimization and business scaling. We partner with advertisers, media buyers, app developers and brands of all sizes, simplifying day-to-day ad operations. This allows our clients to prioritize strategic growth and free themselves from the hassle of traffic procurement and publisher liaison.

We are an online advertising tech firm dedicated to helping advertisers meet performance and branding objectives in mobile affiliate marketing. Supported by our self-owned ADX, SSP and DSP systems, as well as API-connected global developer affiliate platforms, we are capable of delivering high conversion rates and low fraud traffic on both Android and iOS devices. We have accumulated abundant experience in betting, casino and gaming verticals, with over 100 successful cooperation cases, more than 150 traffic sources and 900 partnered advertisers.

Once we obtain your campaign links, we will prioritize your offers via EPC and RTB access to acquire high-quality traffic in bulk. We also conduct real-time traffic optimization based on data feedback. Besides, our in-house anti-fraud system can precisely detect invalid and fraudulent traffic. Its detection results are 60% consistent with Mfilterit and 70% consistent with P360. Given that our cooperative clients attach great importance to fraud prevention, we guarantee to block abnormal traffic at the source.
