ABOUT US:

BorderlessAd is a global AI advertising and media technology company built on over a decade of algorithm research and artificial intelligence. We unite four core capabilities under one platform — worldwide ad delivery, premium global media inventory, true cross-platform reach, and a proprietary AI × Ad engine that fuses deep learning with programmatic advertising. Our team of dozens of top-tier AI scientists and algorithm engineers has spent years engineering deep-learning breakthroughs purpose-built for the advertising industry. Founded in Hong Kong in 2020, we operate across every major region, helping mobile apps, brands, and performance advertisers grow without borders — reaching the right audience on every device, every screen, and every market.

Drawing on deep ad-tech expertise, we provide end-to-end solutions spanning campaign deployment, real-time optimization, and business scaling. We cover CPI, CPA, and CPS advertising models, partnering with advertisers, media buyers, app developers, and brands of all sizes to simplify daily operations and unlock strategic growth — free from the constraints of traditional traffic procurement.

We are an online advertising tech firm dedicated to helping advertisers meet performance and branding objectives in mobile affiliate marketing. Supported by our self-owned ADX, SSP, and DSP stack, plus API-connected global developer affiliate platforms, we deliver high conversion rates and low-fraud traffic on Android, iOS, and CTV worldwide. Our platform automatically integrates with leading attribution partners — AppsFlyer, Adjust, Branch, Kochava, Singular — supporting CTA (Click-Through), VTA (View-Through), and multi-touch attribution models for precise tracking and analytics across every campaign. We have served 100+ successful cases across betting, casino, and gaming verticals, with 150+ traffic sources and 900+ partnered advertisers.

Once we obtain your campaign links, we will prioritize your offers via EPC and RTB access to acquire high-quality traffic in bulk. We also conduct real-time traffic optimization based on data feedback. In addition, our in-house anti-fraud system can precisely detect invalid and fraudulent traffic. Our detection results show 60% consistency with Mfilterit and 70% with P360. Given that our partners place great importance on fraud prevention, we guarantee to block abnormal traffic at the source.
